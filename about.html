About Me

Hello! I’m Balu Kosuri, a writer who enjoys exploring the intersection of technology and storytelling. Over the years, I’ve penned reflective essays, travelogues, and technical guides, all driven by curiosity and a love for clear communication.

This website is designed to be clean and distraction-free so that my words can take center stage. As design experts remind us, keeping a portfolio uncluttered with clear, legible fonts helps readers focus on the writing itself. Minimal layouts also reduce cognitive load and improve usability.

When I’m not writing, you’ll probably find me sipping coffee, reading about design trends, or planning my next adventure.
